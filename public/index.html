Our Story

Experience Our
Patient-Centred Care

We are a 50-bed health facility established in 1968. Over the years, St. Nicholas Hospital has expanded to become a first-class healthcare provider and the preferred referral medical facility for both local and international organisations.

We maintain our reputation by being internationally accredited by the Council for Health Services Accreditation of Southern Africa (COHSASA). Our renal transplant unit was the first in Nigeria and West Africa to perform successful kidney transplants.
