Our Story

A Hospital Built on
Purpose & Firsts

For over half a century, St. Nicholas Hospital has been at the forefront of providing patient-focused care and exceptional clinical services to the people of Nigeria from Lagos. The original St. Nicholas Hospital was a converted dwelling house, registered as a private hospital on 29th July, 1952.

From its inception it admitted mainly obstetrics, gynaecology and paediatric cases — thus the name St. Nicholas, being the patron Saint of women and children. We provide a broad range of services which cut across various medical fields, the ultimate aim of which is to take care of you and support you and your family through what may be a distressing period.

We foster, promote and practice ethical and evidence-based medicine. Our staff members are dedicated and committed to delivering professional services and outstanding hospitality, provided for all individuals in accordance with their needs and with respect for cultural diversity, tribe, religion, nationality, age, and disability — without regard for financial status.

Technology & Accreditation

World-Class Technology,
International Standards

COHSASA Accredited

Internationally accredited by the Council for Health Services Accreditation of Southern Africa (COHSASA), holding us to global standards of patient safety and care quality.

C-Arm Machine

We operate the latest model C-arm imaging scanner intensifier — used for fluoroscopic imaging during orthopaedic procedures, providing real-time visualisation for precise surgical outcomes.

Therapeutic Apheresis

Our latest Therapeutic Apheresis machine selectively removes targeted abnormal substances from blood circulation — treating complex conditions where pathogenic components cause morbidity.

Busiest Dialysis Centre

Our dialysis centre is one of the busiest and most affordable in Lagos. We have dialysed over 3,724 patients since 2000, with our physicians leading diagnosis and treatment of renal disease.

WACP & NPMCN Accredited

A Centre for postgraduate training in family medicine, accredited by the West African College of Physicians (WACP) and the National Postgraduate College of Nigeria (NPMCN).

100+ Double-Qualified Nurses

Our highly skilled medical staff includes full-time and visiting consultants, Registrars, Medical Officers, House Officers — and over 100 double-qualified nurses maintaining a strong focus on patient safety.
