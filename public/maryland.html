About This Branch

Multi-Specialist Care in Maryland

St. Nicholas Hospital Maryland is our full-service multi-specialist branch serving the heart of mainland Lagos. With the ever-increasing incidence of lifestyle diseases such as diabetes, hypertension, stroke, and heart disease, early detection and proactive management are essential.

Our Maryland branch offers specialist wellness packages — WELLMAN and WELLWOMAN — specially designed to suit individual needs. These packages detect your current health status and screen all relevant biomedical parameters to identify risk factors, empowering you to take the right steps before conditions worsen.
