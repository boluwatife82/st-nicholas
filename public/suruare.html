About This Branch

World-Class Healthcare Right in Your Neighbourhood

St. Nicholas Hospital Surulere brings over 70 years of medical excellence to the heart of one of Lagos's most vibrant communities. Whether you're a busy professional, a growing family, or caring for elderly loved ones — we're here to serve you with the same internationally-accredited care that has made St. Nicholas a trusted name across Nigeria.

Our Surulere branch offers round-the-clock access to specialist care. No appointment is necessary for urgent cases — our doors are open 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. Walk in knowing you will be seen by qualified, compassionate healthcare professionals committed to your wellbeing.
